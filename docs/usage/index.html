Getting Access¶

This site is open to registered users only. However, since some of the information available here is sensitive access to those pages is restricted to current HAFFA members and authorized guests.

Logging In¶

Click on the Login link at the top of this page. You will be asked to provide your registered user name and password to access to the site.

For new users, click on the register link on the Login page to sign up. You will need to pick a user name and password, and provide a valid email address. Passwords are not stored directly, so you need to remember your password. If you forget your password, click on Forgot Password and a change password request can be sent to the registered email address.

Once you complete the registration step, you will have limited guest-only access to the site. After the club secretary validates your membership status, access rights will be raised to full member access.
